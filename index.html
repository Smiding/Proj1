History

Westermalm HC is a local handball team that was founded in 1902 for the purpose of playing handball as a recreational activity. While the team may not have achieved any notable championships or accolades, they still take pride in their sport and enjoy playing it for fun. Despite not playing in a high division or having a professional status, the team still values teamwork, sportsmanship, and having a good time playing the sport they love.

Play with us

Playing handball with Westermalm HC is a fun and enjoyable experience for several reasons. First and foremost, the team members are passionate about the sport and play with a positive attitude. They welcome players of all skill levels and prioritize creating a welcoming and inclusive environment. Additionally, the team values sportsmanship and fair play, so players can feel confident that games will be played with respect and integrity.
